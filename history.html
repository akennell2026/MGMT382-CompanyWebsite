About Us

The Creation of Stride Glide

Company Overview

Who we are: An innovative sports apparel company designing high-quality and durable sportswear.

Mission: Enhancing athlete performance and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Vision: Creating sustainable, comfortable, and fashionable sports apparel for all.

Core Values: Innovation, Sustainability, Comfort, Durability.

Company History

Founded in 1980, Stride Glide started with ultra-comfortable running shoes. Founder Alex Foster, struggling to find durable running shoes, created the first "Boost" model, which became widely popular. As demand grew, Stride Glide expanded to shoes for various sports like tennis, basketball, and soccer. By 2005, it was the #1 shoe sponsor for professional athletes. In 2008, the company diversified into sports apparel, further solidifying its market leadership. Stride Glide continues investing in R&D to enhance performance and durability.
